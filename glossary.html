Project: Project name

Form: ODK generated form used for the survey

Country: National administrative level. Country of the specified project.

Season: Agricultural season. Varies with different countries

Enumerator: Survey personnel charged with data collection

Household: An agricultural household where at least one member of the household is operating a holding or when the household head, reference person or main income earner is economically active in agriculture..

ENID: Enumerator identification number.

HHID: Household identification number.

FDID: Field identification number..

TLID: Trial identification number..

Complete: Submitted with correct information within required time period.

Missing Details: Submitted but missing information.

Overdue: Submission overdue and no information submitted.

Future Event: Future data collection event, due date not yet passed and information not yet submitted.

Quality Checks: More information on the completeness of the data submitted.

Register Household: When the household was registered by enumerator. obtained from Register or Verify Household form.

Register Field: When Field was assigned. Obtained from Assign Field, Trial, and/or Plot form

Field Description: When detailed fied description was done. Obtained from Describe Field form

Soil Sampling: When soil sample was colected. Obtained from Collect Soil Sample form

Record Crop Variety: When planting main crop data was collected. Obtained from Record Trial Management form .

Germination Count: When count of the number of plants in plot was done. Obtained from Measure Plot form

Top Dressing: When fertilizer application 2 was done. Obtained from Record Trial Management form.

PD Scoring: Pests and diseases scoring. Obtained from Measure Plot form.

Weeding:When weeding events were done. Obtained from Record Trial Management form.

Household Survey: When detailed description of the household was done. Obtained from Describe Household form.

Plant Sampling: When plant sampling was done.Obtained from Measure Plot form.

Harvest: When harvesting events were done. Varies for different crops.Obtained from Measure Plot form.
